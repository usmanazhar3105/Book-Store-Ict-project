FICTION


Imaginative stories with invented characters, settings, and plots, exploring themes like love, courage, and identity.



NON-FICTION


Books based on real events and facts, aimed at informing, educating, or entertaining on subjects like history, science, or self-improvement.



SCIENCE


Books that explain scientific concepts and discoveries, ranging from physics to biology, often making complex topics accessible.



MYSTERY


Stories centered around solving puzzles, crimes, or secrets, with suspenseful plots and twists, often featuring detectives or sleuths.
